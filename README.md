# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (SQL)

This project involves a detailed analysis of sales data for a coffee shop operating in multiple locations. Using **SQL**, I processed thousands of transaction records to uncover key business metrics, such as monthly revenue growth, peak sales hours, and top-performing product categories.

- What are the top-selling products by quantity and revenue?

- **Sales Metrics:** Calculated Total Sales, Total Orders, and Total Quantity Sold for different time periods.

- **Product Intelligence:** Categorized sales by product type and size (Small, Medium, Large) to understand customer preferences.
